OUR STORY

Scents by Sara was founded to honour real bodies, meaningful ritual, and scent as emotional architecture.

FOUNDER NOTE

A BRAND BUILT FROM LIVED EXPERIENCE

I created Scents by Sara to reframe the way women see themselves. Every sculpted candle is designed to celebrate the body as it is: changing, strong, and worthy of softness.

From hand-pouring to scent curation, each piece is crafted as a quiet ritual object, something that can hold memory, mood, and intention inside everyday spaces.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

THE VALUES THAT SHAPE EVERY COLLECTION

Embodied Confidence

Design that celebrates natural form, texture, and the stories written on our bodies.

Scent Science

Fragrance profiles developed to support mood, grounding, and intentional routines.

Ethical Craft

Small-batch production, vegan wax, and conscious material choices at every stage.

Community-Led

Real customer stories and lived narratives guide the product and content direction.
